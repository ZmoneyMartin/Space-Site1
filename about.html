About Us

We believe space should be for everyone—not just career astronauts. Our mission is to give ordinary people the once-in-a-lifetime chance to travel beyond Earth. With our two-month training program, anyone can prepare to become an astronaut and spend a full week in space aboard one of our rocketships.

Our Story

We started this company with a dream: to make space travel accessible. What began as a family vision has grown into a program built with support from NASA and SpaceX. Together, we’re proving that the future of exploration isn’t limited to professionals—it’s open to anyone brave enough to take the leap.

Our Campus

Our home base is in Tigard, Oregon, where we operate a state-of-the-art 5-acre training facility. The campus has everything our future astronauts need to get ready for launch, including:

  • A swim center for underwater training
  • Two fully equipped gyms
  • Modern dorms that can house up to 500 people at a time

Here, our instructors—many of them former astronauts and aerospace experts—live and work alongside trainees, creating a hands-on environment where preparation and community come together.
